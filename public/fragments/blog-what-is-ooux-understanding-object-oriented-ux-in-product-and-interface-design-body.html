What Is OOUX? Understanding Object-Oriented UX in Product and Interface Design

What Is OOUX and Why Should UX/UI Designers Care?
In the video below, we introduce the concept of OOUX (Object-Oriented User Experience) and explain why it’s becoming a key approach in modern product design. OOUX is all about focusing on the core objects users interact with, rather than just screens or flows. This method helps designers create more intuitive, scalable, and user-friendly digital products.

How Does OOUX Improve UX/UI and Product Design?
The video answers essential questions for anyone working in UX, UI, or product design.

What are the benefits of using OOUX compared to traditional UX methods?

What Will You Learn from This Video?
You’ll gain practical insights into applying OOUX principles to your own projects. The video covers the basics of object mapping, shows how to break down complex systems into manageable components, and offers tips for integrating OOUX into your UX/UI workflow.
This content is ideal for UX/UI designers, product managers, and anyone interested in building digital products that are both user-centric and future-proof.
